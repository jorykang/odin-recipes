Lasagna

Description

Tender sheets of pasta, a creamy ricotta cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce are all topped with more cheese and baked until browned and bubbly

It is the best lasagna recipe!

Ingredients

For the meat sauce

  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 500 gms lean minced beef
  • 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbs oregano
  • 200 gms chestnut mushrooms

To assemble the lasagna

  • 9 or 10 dry lasagna sheets
  • 200 gms cheddar cheese, grated
  • 50 gms mozzarella cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Place olive oil in deep frying pan and heat for 1 minute
  2. Brown the onions and garlic
  3. Add the minced beef and cook until brown
  4. Add the mushrooms and cook for 3 to 4 minutes
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, salt and pepper
  6. Bring sauce to simmer and then lower the heat to maintain a low simmer
  7. Cook for 30 minutes while stirring regularly
  8. Remove sauce from heat
  9. Preheat oven to 200 degrees celsius
  10. Place first layer of lasagna sheets into bottom of dish
  11. Add some sauce on top and then sprinkle with small amount of cheddar cheese
  12. Repeat previous step until you get to the top of the dish or run out of sauce
  13. Sprinkle the mozzarella on the top layer along with any remaining cheddar cheese
  14. Place the dish into the oven and bake for 45 minutes or until golden brown
